SEEDS DATA VISUALIZATION

Energy consumption visualized.

Energy usage burns coal, crude oil and other fossil fuels. If people were given an easy to use tool to understand how much energy they're using it could motivate them to reduce energy. This in turn will protect the air and prevent climate change. Our specific goal with this project was provide an interface for a user to get a deep understanding of their energy usage.
